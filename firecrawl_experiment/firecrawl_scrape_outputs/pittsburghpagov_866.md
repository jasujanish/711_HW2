# LIR Members

The Board shall be [composed](mailto:) of not more than five (5) members appointed by the Mayor, and approved by City Council. All Board members shall be residents of the City of Pittsburgh, and at least two (2) members shall have experience working with community advocacy groups or community development corporations, and at least one (1) member shall have two (2) or more years' experience working in business licensing and building code inspections. Board membership is an unpaid position. The current members are:

- Steve Mazza (Chair)
- Julie Asciolla
- Lauren Byrne Connelly
- Alan Hord
- Tyi-Sanna Jones
