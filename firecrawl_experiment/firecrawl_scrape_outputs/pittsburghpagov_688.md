# Compliance Resources

The Office of Equal Protection has prepared these resources to assist businesses in getting into or staying in legal compliance and to help workers understand their rights and meet their obligations under the Paid Sick Days Act. [If you need to request a live webinar with Q&A for a group, please complete this form.](https://forms.office.com/g/XgkWAQUu3Z)
