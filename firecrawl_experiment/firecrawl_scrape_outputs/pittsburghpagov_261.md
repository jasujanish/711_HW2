# Sign Up for Community Development Grant Updates

Fill out this form to receive email updates about the City of Pittsburgh's **Public Service** and **Neighborhood Economic Development** grants. Please note that these funds are only available to nonprofits that provide services to low-and-moderate income City of Pittsburgh residents. Email the Office of Management and Budget's Community Development Division at [community.development@pittsburghpa.gov](mailto:community.development@pittsburghpa.gov) with any questions.
