# Get Involved

For questions on The Homewood Community Plan, contact Christopher Corbett, neighborhood planner, at [Christopher.Corbett@pittsburghpa.gov](mailto:Christopher.Corbett@pittsburghpa.gov) or 412-393-0160.

The plan will be presented to **Planning Commission for Briefing on Tuesday, March 10 at 1 p.m.** This meeting is open to the public, but no public comment will be allowed.

The plan will be presented to **Planning Commission a second time for Hearing and Action on Tuesday, March 24 at 2 p.m.** This meeting is open to the public, and community members are invited to attend and voice their support or opposition to the plan.
