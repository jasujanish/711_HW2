Request an Interpreter, Translation, or Accommodation
To request an interpreter, translated materials, or disability-related, religious, or other accommodation in order to access the Pittsburgh Commission on Human Relations (“PghCHR”) and its services, please contact us by phone at 412-255-2600 or by email at human.relations@pittsburghpa.gov.
