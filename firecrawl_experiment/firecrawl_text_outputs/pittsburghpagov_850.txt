Stars at Riverview Jazz Series

Saturdays, June 7 - July 26 \| 7 - 8:30 PM
Riverview Park \| Riverview Ave, Observatory Hill
In 2025, "Stars at Riverview" boasts out of this world jazz Saturday evenings at Riverview Park. Bring a lawn chair or spread a blanket to witness the stellar talents of local musicians on Observatory Hill. Rotating food trucks available for purchase each week. FREE TO ATTEND! After the concert, stay for Dollar Bank Cinema in the Park.
Series presented by Citizens and supported by the Allegheny Regional Asset District (RAD)
