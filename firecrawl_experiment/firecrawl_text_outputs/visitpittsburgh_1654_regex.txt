On Monday, June 30, 2025 the passionate Pittsburgh crowd will again get the chance to see history as the WWE brings two superstar-studded events in one night to PPG Paints Arena for WWE Monday Night RAW and Friday Night SmackDown.
When & Where
The WWE Monday Night RAW and Friday Night SmackDown will take place on Monday, June 30, 2025 at 5:30 p.m. This event will be held at PPG Paints Arena ( 1001 Fifth Ave).
