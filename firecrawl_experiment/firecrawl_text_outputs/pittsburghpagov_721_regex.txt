Filing Disclosure of Interests
Elected City officials (mayor, controller, members of council) must file a Disclosure of Interests with the City controller on or before January 15th of every year during their term of office. For more on the disclosure of interest see Article 8, Section 801 of the City's Home Rule Charter(PDF, 401KB).
