Park Permits
Most park shelters can be booked online immediately. If you are unable to book online, phone/in-person reservations will be done by appointment only. Please contact dpwfields&amp;parkshelters@pittsburghpa.gov to schedule an appointment.
We accept credit cards, checks and money orders as payment for permits.
