Independent Citizen Police Review Board
The Independent Citizen Police Review receives and investigates complaints of police conduct, holds public hearings, renders findings &amp; recommendations and offers advice on police practices, policies and procedures to the Chief of Police and the Mayor. The Board is comprised of seven volunteer members appointed to four-year terms.
