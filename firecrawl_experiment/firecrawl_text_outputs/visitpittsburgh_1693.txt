Upcoming Events
The Pittsburgh theater scene thrives no matter the season! Whether you’re into the grand elegance of historical landmarks such as the Benedum, Byham and Heinz Hall, or the outrageous Pittsburgh Improv, there’s surely a show for everyone’s enjoyment!
Check out some of the unforgettable drama, laughs, music and dance taking over the Pittsburgh spotlight this season!
