Banquet Hall
The Banquet Hall at Schenley Rink can hold up to 99 guests. Facility Rental: $575.00
To reserve the banquet hall at Schenley Park Skating Rink, call or email: (412) 422-6523 or schenleyrink@pittsburghpa.gov
Ice Time Rentals
Private Ice Time Rentals are available for $75.00/hour.
To make your ice time rental call or email: (412) 422-6523 or schenleyrink@pittsburghpa.gov
Please follow all posted signage while at the Ice Rink.
