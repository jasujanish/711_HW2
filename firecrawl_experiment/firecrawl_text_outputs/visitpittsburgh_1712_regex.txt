Top Things to Do Between Christmas & New Years in Pittsburgh
Hosting family for the holidays or looking to make the most of your time off?
Pittsburgh is brimming with exciting things to do the week between Christmas and New Year’s. With festive attractions, outdoor adventures, and activities for all ages, you’ll find plenty of ways to make unforgettable memories together.

For the Holiday Enthusiast
Keep the holiday spirit alive, even after Christmas Day, by visiting Pittsburgh’s festive attractions.
Kennywood \| Stroll through a dazzling display of lights, festive music, and holiday cheer at Kennywood’s enchanting Holiday Lights experience.
Phipps Conservatory \| Step into a world of twinkling wonder with themed light displays and lush Winter Flower Show during Holiday Magic.
Beautifully Lit \| This unique self-guided tour of Pittsburgh and the season's light installations highlights stunning lights, their creators, and the rich history woven into the heart of downtown Pittsburgh.
Pittsburgh Crèche \| Marvel at the Pittsburgh Crèche, a stunning life-size nativity scene and the only one of its kind in the U.S.
Pittsburgh Zoo & Aquarium \| Explore Wild Illuminations, a spectacular lantern experience that transforms the Pittsburgh Zoo into a glowing wonderland.

For Families & Kids
Make memories with family-friendly activities that keep kids engaged and entertained all week long.
Carnegie Museum of Art \| Admire captivating art displays and programs designed to inspire young imaginations.
Daniel G. and Carole L. Kamin Science Center \| Enrich your understanding of science through hands-on exhibits and planetarium shows.
Children’s Museum of Pittsburgh \| Take advantage of the special extended hours during holiday break to fill your little one’s day with imaginative activities and exhibits.
National Aviary \| Encounter tropical birds, penguins, and more in immersive exhibits perfect for curious little ones.
Give your kids an educational holiday break with day camps at many of our museums, filled with hands-on learning and fun.
For the Culture Seeker
Immerse yourself in Pittsburgh’s rich arts and culture to start the new year inspired.
The Andy Warhol Museum \| Explore the iconic works of Pittsburgh-born pop artist Andy Warhol in his namesake museum.
The Frick Pittsburgh \| Celebrate the season and take in the cheerful atmosphere of a home decked for the holidays on a specialty tour of Clayton.
Heinz History Center \| Learn about Pittsburgh’s vibrant history through interactive exhibits. All kids ages 17 and under receive FREE admission to both museums through Dec. 31, 2024.
Mattress Factory Museum of Contemporary Art \| Experience thought-provoking installations, videos, and performance art.
Pittsburgh Ballet Theatre \| Catch one of the season’s final performances of The Nutcracker by the Pittsburgh Ballet Theatre.

For the Outdoor Adventurer
Embrace the winter chill with outdoor activities that will warm you up while showcasing Pittsburgh’s scenic beauty.
The Rink at PPG Place \| Glide across the ice in the heart of Downtown Pittsburgh.
Seven Springs Mountain Resort \| Hit the slopes for a thrilling day of skiing or snowboarding.
Schenley Park \| Take a serene winter hike through Schenley Park’s picturesque trails and frosty landscapes.
For the Foodie
Satisfy your cravings with a self-guided tour of Pittsburgh’s iconic eats, craft beverages, and hidden culinary gems.
15 Foods You Can Only Get in Pittsburgh \| Discover the city’s must-try foods, from pierogies to the legendary sandwiches at Primanti Bros.
Pittsburgh: A Craft Beverage Destination \| Make your way through the city’s vibrant craft beer, cider, and distillery offerings.
Top Pierogi Places in Pittsburgh \| Dive into Pittsburgh’s pierogi culture by visiting top spots serving this local favorite.
For more inspiration, explore all of our foodie guides.
For the Relaxation Seeker
Unwind and recharge with wellness experiences designed to soothe your body and mind.
Nemacolin \| Treat yourself to a luxury wellness retreat and rejuvenating spa treatments.
Salt of the Earth Himalayan Salt Cave \| Enjoy the calming benefits of halotherapy in a serene Himalayan salt cave.
Levity Float Studio \| Float away your stress in a tranquil sensory deprivation tank.
Ready to pack more joy into your holiday? Explore our event calendar to uncover more events and activities happening throughout the week.
