Exploring the natural world, past and present, through historic collections and wonder-filled galleries. Carnegie Museum of Natural History is a place of world-class scientific inquiry and daily discovery. By displaying nature’s wonders, its galleries and collections spark conversation about the inherent worth of nature and inspire a new generation of scientists and citizen scientists to conserve the planet and its many wonders.
