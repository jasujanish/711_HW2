Pickle Riding
Inspired by the classic mechanical bull! Riders can climb aboard the giant mechanical pickle and try to hang on as it bucks, spins, and wiggles with unpredictable power. Rides cost $12 and riders must be over 18.

Li’l Gherkins KidsPlay Activity Area
Enjoy hands-on fun with activities presented by Chick-fil-A and the Pittsburgh Pirates. Kids can get crafty by making their very own pickle button, and try their luck at pickle-themed carnival games. It’s a perfect spot for pint-sized pickle lovers to play and create!
When is it?
Friday & Saturday: 12 – 10 p.m.
Sunday: 12 – 6 p.m.
Where is it?
Allegheny Landing
What Activities will be available?
There will be a free button-making craft table and carnival games including: pickle jar toss, pickle duck pond, pickle bean bag toss, pickle derby race
How much does it cost?
Activities under the KidsPlay tent are free.
The pickle themed carnival games are $2 per play. Carnival game tickets can be purchased onsite- no reservation necessary.
Li’l Gherkins Activity Area Guidelines:
Li’l gherkins must be accompanied by an adult, and adults must be accompanied by a li’l gherkin
One craft per child, ages 14 and under
No food or beverages at the craft table
Please park your stroller outside of the tent
